Top 10 Most Popular F1 Drivers in 2024

The world of Formula 1 is made richer by an impressive line-up of drivers whose great skills, drive, and genuine love for this sport have mesmerized not only the fans but also people all over the world. The record-breaking dominance of Max Verstappen and the unwavering tenacity of Lewis Hamilton have not only pushed the boundaries at speed through precision on track but also seen these drivers re-write the Legends Book in Motorsport Their Icons.

In conjunction with an insatiable drive to drive that has continuously pushed the envelope of what is possible behind the wheel and relentless pursuit of perfection, they have become legends of motorsport commanding respect not only from fans but fellow competitors alike. Here we will discuss the most popular drivers drivers in F1.

  • Max Verstappen – Red Bull: The ultimate man-of-the-moment in F1, Verstappen's dominance in recent seasons has solidified his status as the poster boy of the sport. His exceptional performance and consistency have made him a force to be reckoned with on the track.

  • Lewis Hamilton – Mercedes: The most successful driver in the history of F1, Hamilton's unparalleled achievements make him a standout figure. Despite the challenges posed by the lackluster Mercedes car, his skill and determination continue to set him apart.

  • Fernando Alonso – Aston Martin: With over two decades of experience, Alonso's legendary race craft and resilience make him a formidable competitor. His ability to consistently deliver strong performances at the front solidifies his place among the top drivers in the sport.

  • Lando Norris – McLaren: A fan favorite known for his exceptional performances, Norris has been a standout driver, showcasing his talent and determination. With his eye on leading McLaren's charge, he remains a strong contender in the F1 lineup.

  • Carlos Sainz – Ferrari: Sainz, with his strong race pace and consistent performances, has proven himself as a force to be reckoned with. His podium finishes and unwavering determination make him a key figure in the F1 lineup.

  • George Russell – Mercedes: Russell's swift rise up the grid and strong performances have positioned him as a driver to watch. With his transition to become the main driver at Mercedes, his potential for further success is evident.

  • Charles Leclerc – Ferrari: Bursting onto the scene with strong performances, Leclerc's driving prowess and pole positions highlight his potential for greatness. His resilience and impressive driving style continue to earn him recognition in the F1 lineup.

  • Sergio Perez – Red Bull: A reliable team player known for his solid performances, Perez's ability to excel on street circuits and contribute to Red Bull's success makes him a valuable asset in the F1 lineup.

  • Oscar Piastri – McLaren: Impressive performances at junior levels and a strong debut season in F1 have positioned Piastri as a rising star. His potential for further success and standout performances make him a driver to watch.

  • Valtteri Bottas – Stake: Despite a recent decline in performance, Bottas's history of being a reliable team player and his success with Mercedes earn him a spot among the top drivers in F1.
