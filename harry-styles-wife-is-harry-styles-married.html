Harry Styles Wife: Is Harry Styles Married?

Harry Styles Wife: Is Harry Styles Married?: Harry Styles, officially known as Harry Edward Styles is an English singer and songwriter born on February 1, 1994.

He developed a love for music at a younger age and as a child, he sang covers on a karaoke machine that his grandfather gave him with the first song he recorded being Elvis Presley’s, ‘The Girl of My Best Friend’.

Styles attended the Holmes Chapel Comprehensive School, where he was the lead singer for the band White Eskimo, which won a local Battle of the Bands competition.

He kicked off his musical career in 2010 when he auditioned as a solo contestant for the seventh series of the British televised singing competition, The X Factor.

Following his elimination from the singing competition, four others who were also eliminated were put together to form a band named ‘One Direction’ to compete in the X Factor Groups category.

The band began to gain considerable popularity in the UK, and eventually reached the final of The X Factor and finished in third place.

The band, One Direction went on to become one of the best-selling boy groups of all time before going on an indefinite hiatus in 2016.

During the band’s hiatus, Harry Styles released his self-titled debut solo album in 2017. The album debuted at number one in the UK and the US and was one of the world’s top-ten best-selling albums of the year, while its lead single, “Sign of the Times”, topped the UK Singles Chart.

Harry Styles has risen through the ranks to become one of the most sought-after acts in the entertainment space. His music has been described as soft rock, pop, and rock, with elements of folk and Britpop.

Harry Styles has received various accolades, including two Brit Awards, three Grammy Awards, an Ivor Novello Award, and three American Music Awards.

Apart from his career as a musician, he has also starred in several films including; Dunkirk, Don’t Worry Darling, and My Policeman, among others. He’s also known for his fashion life and is the first man to appear solo on the cover of Vogue.

Harry Styles Wife: Is Harry Styles Married?

We are not very sure whether the award-winning singer is married or not. However, he has been linked with a couple of women.

From November 2011 to January 2012, Harry Styles dated television presenter Caroline Flack. He briefly dated American singer, Taylor Swift later in 2012.

From 2017 to 2018, Styles was in a relationship with French-American model, Camille Rowe, who inspired his 2019 album ‘Fine Line’.

From January 2021 to November 2022, Styles was in a relationship with actress and director Olivia Wilde.
