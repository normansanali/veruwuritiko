Meet Tom Ainsley, Who Played Charlie in “How I Met Your Father”

Seeing positive reactions from the viewers, the makers of the show decided to release the second installment, which has been streaming on Hulu since January 24, 2023, with a new episode coming every week, with the last episode to be aired on June 6.

When was Tom Ainsley Born?

Tom Ainsley (age 32) was born on December 18, 1990, in a Christian family in Scarborough, North Yorkshire, England, UK. Growing up in Scarborough, Tom got curious about acting during middle school when he got to be part of a stage show. By the time Ainsley completed high school, he had already decided to pursue acting as a career and started taking steps in the right direction.
