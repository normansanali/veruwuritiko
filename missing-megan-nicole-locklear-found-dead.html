Missing Megan Nicole Locklear Found Dead At 404 S. Chippewa St, What Happened ?

Megan Nicole Locklear, a resident of Lumberton, was identified as the deceased.

According to a prepared statement by the Lumberton Police Department, Locklear was reported missing on February 8.

The North Carolina Office of the Chief Medical Examiner informed Lumberton police on Thursday that the lady discovered deceased at 404 S. Chippewa St. on April 7 had been identified.

According to Lumberton police, in the vicinity of the location where the reporting party claimed Locklear was last seen on or about Jan. 19, police carried out door-to-door searches of abandoned dwellings.

In an effort to ascertain how she passed away and the precise circumstances of her passing, Lumberton police are currently looking into her death. Police are also awaiting the results of the autopsy.
