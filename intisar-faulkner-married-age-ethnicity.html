Intisar Faulkner Wiki: Age, Family & Ethnicity

Her Education Details

After completing high school, Intisar attended Missouri State University in 2013. There, she graduated with a BS degree in Broadcast Journalism at age 22.

While at college, Faulkner was a member of the MSU Sugar Bears Dance Team.

Moreover, she earned a Master of Arts degree in Communication and Media Studies from there.

Her Journey To WIS-TV

Intisar began her media career as a news anchor at The E.W. Scripps Company, a media company in Cincinnati, Ohio. There she worked for nearly two years until 2019.

Moreover, she was an anchor and reporter at Summit Media Group in Springfield, Missouri. There, she worked as a radio anchor and reporter at KTTS News.


In 2019, she joined WTOK-TV as the anchor for the station’s morning show, “Good Morning Meridian.” She was involved in anchoring and producing programs in the network.

In June 2021, she joined a dual NBC/CW-affiliated WIS-TV. She now serves as an alert desk and co-anchor for WIS Sunrise weekdays and anchors the noon program.
