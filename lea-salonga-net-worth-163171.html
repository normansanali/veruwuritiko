Lea Salonga Net Worth

Lea Salonga is a Filipina actress and singer born on February 22, 1971 in Manila, Philippines. She is best known for her role as Kim in the musical Miss Saigon and for providing the singing voices for Mulan and Princess Jasmine in the Disney animated films Mulan and Aladdin. She has become a beloved figure in the entertainment industry and is widely recognized for her talent.
