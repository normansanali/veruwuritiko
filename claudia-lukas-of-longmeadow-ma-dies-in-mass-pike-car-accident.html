Claudia Lukas of Longmeadow, MA dies in Mass Pike car accident

Longmeadow, MA – The Longmeadow, Massachusetts community mourns the sudden and tragic loss of Claudia Lukas, who passed away unexpectedly following a multi-vehicle collision on the Massachusetts Turnpike, near Hopkinton. The collision, which occurred on Wednesday afternoon, November 15, resulted in injuries that proved fatal for Claudia Lukas.

Reports indicate that the collision took place around 1:35 p.m. on Interstate 90, specifically at mile marker 106. One of the vehicles involved carried both the driver and Claudia Lukas as a passenger. Emergency responders transported them to the MetroWest Medical Center in Framingham.

The circumstances surrounding this devastating incident have left the community reeling from the unexpected loss. Claudia Lukas’ family intends to share information about her obituary and funeral arrangements, offering an opportunity for friends, loved ones, and the community to come together, honor her memory, and bid farewell to a cherished individual whose life was tragically cut short in this accident.
