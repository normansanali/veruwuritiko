Suzanne Cryer 6 Things To Know About The American Actress

From stage to TV and then to films, Suzanne Cryer’s career profile is a testament to her talent and versatility. She has been in the industry for nearly three decades and boasts of impressive acting credits. A New Yorker, Suzanne Rossell Cryer was welcomed into the world on January 13, 1967. She is one of the two daughters born to Dick and Libby Cryer.

Though her hometown is Rochester, the actress grew up in Connecticut and had her basic education at Greenwich High School. She then proceeded to study English Literature at Yale University. Cryer later honed her theatrical skills at the Yale School of Drama. While there may be information readily available about Cryer’s professional life, the same cannot be said of her personal life. Find out interesting facts you never knew about the veteran actress below.

1. She Shines More on The Small Screen

Suzanne Cryer’s earliest acting gigs can be traced back to her days at the Yale School of Drama where she performed with the Utah Shakespeare Festival. She has an impressive list of credits on stage. Notably are The Rivals produced by Hartford Stage, Don Juan produced by Baltimore Center Stage, Mark Taper Forum’s Arcadia, and Proposals by Neil Simon.

Suzanne Cryer made her small screen debut in 1992 with a minor role in the hit series Law & Order. She has since followed up with guest appearances and recurring roles in popular series such as New York News (1995), Lifestories: Families in Crisis and Caroline in the City both in 1996, Seinfeld (1997), Conrad Bloom (1998), Two Guys, a Girl and a Pizza Place (1998 – 2001), and It’s Like, You Know…(1991 – 2001).

Suzanne had a very busy TV career between 2001 and 2010. Fans can also recognize Cryer for her roles in Frasier (2002 – 2003), and Dr. Vegas (2004). In the year 2005, she appeared in The King of Queens, ER, Inconceivable, and Out of Practice. The next year, she also landed notable roles in Bones, CSI: Miami, and Shark. The year 2007 was no different for Cryer who featured in Grey’s Anatomy, Desperate Housewives, Criminal Minds, and Veronica Mars.

Suzanne Cryer’s other notable TV credits are Sex and the City as well as The Starter Wife both in 2008, Nip/Tuck, American Dad! and Dexter all in 2009, Shameless (2014), Lucifer (2017), and The Fosters (2013 – 2018). She has remained a recurring character on Silicon Valley since 2015.

2. Her Movie Roles 

She made her silver screen debut in the 1992 flick Vaudeville. Two years later, Cryer followed up with another role in the short movie Some Folks Call It a Sling Blade. Her other notable film credits are Wag the Dog (1997), Wilbur Falls, and My Mother Dreams the Satan’s Disciples in New York, both released in 1998. Fans can recognize Suzanne Cryer for her outstanding roles in Friends & Lovers (1999), The Happiest Day of Life (2007), Teen Beach Movie (2013), 10 Cloverfield Lane, and its 2018 sequel The Cloverfield Paradox.

3. She’s a Doting Wife and Mom

Suzanne Cryer is one of those stars who prefer to keep details of their personal lives under the radar. However, she is happily married to her heartthrob, Gregory Luke. There is a dearth of information about the couple’s romance from the outset but it is no secret that they have been married since the year 2000. Suzanne and Gregory’s union has produced a daughter and two sons. While there is little or no information readily available about the actress’s family life, it is apparent that she has so far, done a good job at juggling her busy career while maintaining a balance at the homefront.

4. Social Media Presence

Cryer has an active social media life. While she may not be present on all popular social networking sites, she connects with fans on a more personal note and has amassed a strong fanbase on her Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram accounts.

5. What Is Her Net Worth?

Suzanne Cryer boasts of a plethora of credits in a prolific acting career that spans over two decades. While she is perhaps yet to reach the zenith of her career, the actress has successfully carved a niche for herself in the ever-competitive industry and is quite accomplished in her own right. Cryer’s annual income and net worth have not been disclosed but she is presumed to have amassed a decent fortune from her numerous acting gigs and can afford a comfortable lifestyle.

6. Suzanne Cryer’s Body Measurements

Suzanne Cryer has impressive physical features. The brown-haired actress stands at an average height of 5 feet 6 inches. Cryer’s weight and other body measurements have not been disclosed but judging by her looks, it is safe to say the actress has maintained a good physique over the years.
