Naked photos that are meant to be leaked! Real Housewives of Miami star Adriana De Moura takes it all off for a new PETA campaign and Us Weekly has the exclusive first look at the ultra-sexy photos.

Art dealer De Moura is lending a hand — and the rest of her body — to fight for the release of Lolita, an orca at the Miami Seaguarium. Lolita has been confined in a tank since being captured off the coast of Washington in 1970.

“Could you live in a bathtub for 40 years?” De Moura’s campaign reads. In her ad, the Bravo star shows a glimpse of her naked body while posing in a bathtub. In even sexier outtakes from her photo shoot, De Moura lays nude in the tub with her hands covering her breasts and blue water strategically covering her bottom half.

“For me the way she’s been incarcerated for all these years is just wrong on so many levels,” De Moura told NBC Miami in an interview about the campaign. (In De Moura’s home country of Brazil, keeping marine mammals in captivity was outlawed in the ’90s.) “How would you feel if your child was taken away from you captured and used for pure entertainment?”
