Blac Chyna Flaunted Legs in Shimmery Red Gown as She Made Unexpected Appearance at 2020 Grammys

Blac Chyna made a bold appearance in red as she surprised photographers at the red carpet of the Grammy Awards. The rapper sizzled and showed no signs of distress following Rob Kardashians latest allegations against her.

Blac Chyna made a rare appearance at the Grammy Awards and stood out in red on the red carpet. The controversial female rapper glittered and made quite an impression on music’s biggest awards show.

On her Instagram, she shared a slow-mo clip of her sultry look as she posed for photos. She described it as "Beauty at its finest" in her caption.

A BOLD, RARE APPEARANCE

All eyes were on the stunning singer who showed up looking divine in a red gown with long sleeves that fell on one shoulder and two thigh-high slits. She stood out on the red carpet due to the shimmering fabric of her dress.

Chyna made sure fans noticed her intricate tattoos on her thigh and lower leg by posing with her red heels forward and her slits wide open. She also drew attention to her face emphasized by bronzed eyes and pink lips.

The rapper’s hair fell in gentle waves way past her shoulders and smiled for photographers who delighted in her rare appearance. On her Instagram, she shared a slow-mo clip of her sultry look as she posed for photos. She described it as "Beauty at its finest" in her caption. Never mind that she matched the red carpet because she managed to stand out.

In another post, she posed outdoors and wrote, "I’m living my life to the fullest, focus on the positive." We're guessing this is her mantra nowadays considering her recent problems with her ex-fiance, Rob Kardashian and their battle over their daughter, Dream Kardashian.

A NASTY BATTLE

Earlier this month, Kardashian filed new court documents demanding an amendment in their existing joint custody agreement. The reality star accused Chyna of drug and alcohol abuse and an unfit mother for their daughter. He cited Chyna’s lack of attention to Dream’s hygiene and alleged she taught their daughter to twerk naked and act out sexual positions.

Chyna’s camp reportedly denied the accusations and is determined to battle it out in court with Kardashian who’s demanding to reduce Chyna’s time with their daughter to the weekends with a nanny present.

Rob Kardashian and Blac Chyna at the latter's birthday celebration at the Hard Rock Cafe in May 2016. | Photo: Getty Images

THE CALM BEFORE THE STORM

The war between the exes simmered early last year when they seemed to come to a good place in their co-parenting arrangement. Chyna even lauded Kardashian for being a “wonderful father.” He, on the other hand, confirmed that they were active co-parents who had no pending cases in court at the time.

But the tides have turned and the two are seeing another round of hearings with Kardashian’s latest move. We don't expect Chyna to back down and suspect a long battle to ensue.
