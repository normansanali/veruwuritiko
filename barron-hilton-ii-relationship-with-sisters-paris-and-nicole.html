Does Barron Hilton II Actually Get Along With His Famous Sisters Paris And Nicole?

The Hilton family is a family that has spanned over four generations, hailing from New York City and Los Angeles. The family holds a lot of status as A-list socialites. According to The Richest, their wealth comes from the famous Hilton hotel chain founded in 1919 by Conrad Hilton.

Paris Hilton is obviously one of the most famous members of the Hilton family. A household name as a reality star, she's helped maintain the relevance of the Hilton dynasty. However, she does have some less-famous siblings that are just as important to the Hilton regime.

While Paris and Nicole are colossal reality show stars, Barron Hilton has kept the lowest profile of the siblings. He is somewhat famous, mainly due to his food-centric social media posts. Many fans wonder about him and his relationship with his sisters - here's everything to know.

How Much Is Barron Hilton The Second Worth?

Barron certainly had the most private childhood out of all his siblings. Not many details are known about his background and what he was like growing up. The same really can't be said for his siblings, most of all Paris.

According to Biography Mask, Barron was born on November 7th, 1989, making him 32 years old. His exact birthplace is unknown, but he definitely grew up in Los Angeles, California.

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Barron has a net worth of $5 million. He hasn't exactly inherited all of his money, as he has acted in TV shows and movies. Per IMDb, Barron has appeared in 2015's En Passant, a short film he also wrote and directed.

He also appeared in Metamorphosis: Junior Year, and Spring to Winter. According to Kidadl, Barron also has a prestigious role on the board of his family's Hilton company as Senior Vice President.

Barron has made some money as an influencer on social media as well. He typically posts about his family life, but he also will occasionally post about food, horror movies, and fashion. He currently has 1 million followers on Instagram.

According to People, Barron has been married to Tessa von Walderdorff since 2018. For those who are unaware, Walderdorff is a real estate investor, as well as a social media influencer. She's even acted at some points throughout her career and has a couple of writing credits for various German magazines.

Barron's family life including his wife seems great, but how great is it truly? Does he get along with his sisters at all?

Does Barron Hilton Get Along With Nicole Hilton Rothschild?

Paris may be the most famous of Barron's sisters, and the Nicole most people associate her with is Nicole Richie. However, Paris and Barron share another sister named Nicole, who happens to be a part of two famous families.

Nicole Rothschild is yet another socialite in the family. She's also a member of the Rothschild family through her marriage to James Rothschild while maintaining her connection to the Hilton family through her own bloodline.

According to FactsBuddy, Nicole's primary career is as a fashion designer. She has a ton of clothing lines and even some jewelry products under her name.

Nicole also lives a private life, so naturally, there isn't a lot of information known about her relationship with Barron. They have been pictured together at various events, including a few fashion shows. That's really all that's public about their relationship.

Does Barron Hilton Get Along With Paris Hilton?

Paris Hilton has been a lot of things throughout her career. She's been a socialite, model, singer, author, fashion designer, actress, and at one point, a DJ. She was raised in Beverly Hills, California, another great-grandchild of Conrad Hilton.

She is one of today’s most recognizable entrepreneurs and international influencers. She is a pioneer in television, podcasting, and NFTs. She's innovated heavily in reality shows and social media, two platforms that have really grown up since her initial outbreaks in those mediums.

Paris's biggest impact by far has been on the fashion industry. Paris was the queen of the 2000s fashion. During the paparazzi boom of the early 2000s, round-the-clock coverage of her led to her completely revolutionalizing fashion as we know it. The sudden popularity of luxury bags and Ugg boots could not have happened without Hilton. Even the Kardashians in today's world aren't as impactful as Hilton was 20 years ago.

She's even advocated for human rights, something her haters would have never expected her to do. According to Dazed Digital, Paris called for a bill of rights to protect younger people.

Paris is known for her friendships with stars such as Kim Kardashian, but does she get along with her brother Barron?

Once again, due to Barron's private life, their relationship isn't known as well as some other famous siblings. However, according to an article on DoveClove, Paris and Barron trust each other. They've always gotten along pretty well behind the scenes. Barron has always accepted her for who she is, despite some of the more controversial moments in her life.

According to Brides.com, Paris attended Barron's 2018 wedding in the Caribbean. Her relationship with Tessa von Walderdorff is unknown, but things are likely fine.

Paris's relationship with Nicole also appears to be fine, although once again, not a lot has been revealed regarding it.

As mentioned previously, Paris is more known for her sister-like connection to Nicole Richie. Paris and Richie have had a complicated relationship since the 2000s. Paris and Richie went to the same schools and lived in the same neighborhood. They starred in five seasons of The Simple Life together from 2003 to 2007.

According to E!, the two had a major falling-out after the show ended. They would reconcile in 2006. They were last seen together at Hilton’s wedding in November 2021, an event the entire family attended.
