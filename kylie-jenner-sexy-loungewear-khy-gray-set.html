Kylie Jenner Puts a Sexy Spin on Loungewear in an All-Gray Fit

If anyone knows how to make lazy-girl loungewear look sexy, it’s the Kardashian-Jenner clan. Taking a style cue from big sister Kim Kardashian and her buttery Skims pajamas and lounge sets, Kylie Jenner stepped out in a not-yet-released lounge set from her own clothing brand, Khy.

The fit was a stone-gray set set crafted from a stretchy, skintight fabric. The top was slinky and asymmetric, with a short sleeve on one side and a twisty, crisscrossing strap on the other. The design has become a Khy signature; earlier pieces are similarly embellished. The pants of the set looked like high-end flared leggings—sculpting and high-waisted at the top and breezy at the ankles.

BACKGRID

Jenner wore the gray set with black thong sandals and carried a large slouchy black tote bag. She wore her black hair slicked back into a super-neat bun and had on glowy, natural makeup, including a glossy pink lip courtesy of her latest Kylie Cosmetics launch, the Supple Kiss Lip Glaze.

Jenner reposted the photo of her monochrome look from a fan account on her Instagram Story and teased, “@khy soon soon soon.”

More Celebrity News

With her fashion brand, which she launched last fall, Jenner has debuted everything from comfy sweats to denim sets, floor-length dresses, and sporty bodysuits. “Khy is a homage to the limitless possibilities of fashion. We are breaking down barriers and will redefine the meaning of a designer brand, with creativity and quality at a better price,” Jenner said of the label in a press release. “I wanted to make Khy a platform for our customers to have the opportunity to experience fashion, through new designers and brands, but at a more accessible price.”
