Who Is Kassandra Love on the Spectrum? Age Cosplay Instagram and Parents

Who is Kassandra? The Cool cosplayer from Australia was seen in Netflix’s show Love on the Spectrum. 

Kassandra is a passionate cosplayer and anime lover. She has been cosplaying different anime and cartoon characters.

Viewers were introduced to Kassandra Love on the Spectrum, a Netflix original series following individuals on the spectrum to find love. The highly anticipated show aired its first season in July 2020. The second installment of the show has been released on September 21, 2021.

Who is Kassandra From Love on the Spectrum?

Kassandra is a professional cosplayer who loves anime and manga. She describes herself as weird and loves herself that way.

Kassandra is on her quest to find love. Though she says that she has dated in the past and knows what love is. She explains that she has yet to feel the intimate form of love.

She is a talented dance teacher with 20 years of dancing experience. Kassandra opened up on her first date from the show that she has performed in musical theaters and recently performed in Shrek The Musical.

Not much has been revealed about her personal life and physical attributes like height and weight.

The reality Tv star says she often gets questions about her look. That she doesn’t look Autistic. But Kassandra has a perfect answer to those questions. She simply answers one cannot look inside my brain.

She still continues to do cosplays, and it seems like she is also a gamer as per her TikTok video, where she shared her setup.

Kassandra Age Revealed – How Old Is She?

Kassandra is 27 years old. She reveals in the intro part of the show that she felt like she didn’t belong and felt different from others when she was in grade five or six.

Kassandra reveals that someone telling her she was on the Autism spectrum was the best day of her life. It gave her clarity of things she had been wondering about for a long time.

Meet the Cosplayer Kassandra on Instagram

Kassandra Love makes cosplay for herself and her peers alike. Displaying her art through Instagram with 1360 followers. She shares her day-to-day life in the photo-sharing app as well.

She also has a Tiktok account where she does lipsyncs and shares her creations. Kassandra has gained 23.4k followers in the video-sharing app.

Kassandra Parents – Where is she from?

Kassandra Love’s parents haven’t been revealed. Residing in Australia, Kassandra’s parents didn’t mark their presence in the show as well.

However, her friends seemed happy and excited for her to finally start dating again.
