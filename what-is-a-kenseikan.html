What are the things in Byakuya’s hair?

Byakuya has slate gray eyes and long black hair, which he keeps up in intricate white headpieces called kenseikan (牽星箝, Pulled Star Insert; Viz “Pull Star Silence”), which symbolize his nobility as the head of the Kuchiki Clan, wearing three on top of his head and another two on the right side.
