Callum Walsh - Age, Family, Bio

Southpaw super welter fighter best known as 'The King.' He won all five of his first professional fights. He uploads primarily boxing content to his popular kingcallumwalsh Instagram page. He has garnered over 180,000 followers as a result. 

Before Fame

Prior to his professional career, he was a six-time Ireland Youth Champion. He also won a gold medal at the European Championships. 

Trivia

He made his professional fighting debut against Earl Henry on December 10, 2021. He won the fight via knockout.

Family Life

Growing up, he lived with his family in Cork, Ireland. His father first took him to the boxing gym while he was only six years old. 

Associated With

He posted a January 7, 2023, Instagram photo with Freddie Roach in Boston, Massachusetts.
