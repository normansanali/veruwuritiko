FKA Twigs &amp; Shia LaBeouf Reportedly Dating

FKA Twigs, who previously dated Robert Pattinson, and Shia LaBeouf, who is currently still allegedly married to Mia Goth (but are separated) are said to be the newest Hollywood couple. The movie follows a hard-working child actor who works on fixing his relationship with his alcoholic father.
