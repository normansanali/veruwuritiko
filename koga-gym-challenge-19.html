Koga
キョウ Kyou

Koga (Japanese: キョウ Kyou) is a Trainer card. It is part of the Gym Challenge expansion.

Card text

If an attack from a Pokémon with Koga in its name does damage to a Defending Pokémon this turn, that Pokémon is then Poisoned.

Release information

This card was included in the Gym Challenge expansion as both a Holofoil and Non Holofoil card. The Non Holofoil print was first released in the Japanese Challenge from the Darkness expansion.

Trivia

Origin

Koga is one of the Gym Leaders of the Kanto region.
