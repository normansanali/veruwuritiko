DD Osama - Age, Family, Bio

Rapper and hip-hop recording artist who is known for singles such as "Without You," "Dead Opps," and "40s N 9s." His music video for the latter has gained over 10 million views on YouTube. His popularity led to him amassing more than 1.5 million followers on his personal Instagram account.  His real name is David Reyes.

Before Fame

He began rapping in 2021 and releasing music professionally in 2022. He was a featured artist on the song "PARTY IN THE USA."

Trivia

He is a member of the Notti World collective. His music videos have been viewed more than 222 million times on his official YouTube channel.

Family Life

He collaborated on music with his brother Ethan Reyes, known professionally as Notti Osama, who passed away in July 2022. His other brothers are JayKlickin and JStar Balla. He also has a sister named Melz. He has been romantically linked to Alabama Barker. 

Associated With

Both he and Roddy Ricch found fame through social media.
