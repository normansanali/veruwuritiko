Triple H Expresses His Love For WWE Couple Montez Ford &amp; Bianca Belair

WWE’s Chief Content Officer, Triple H has his hands full dealing with the fallout from Vince McMahon sexual assault scandal. Triple H is trying his best to do damage control and handle the negative impact on the wrestling company from McMahon’s controversial past. However, despite his busy schedule he took the time out to share his praise for WWE power couple, Motez Ford and Bianca Belair.

Triple H congratulated the couple after they announced their new reality series on Hulu. The WWE focused series, “Love & WWE: Bianca & Montez” will revolve around Bianca and Motez as they navigate through the world of wrestling and love. Following the announcement of the show, Triple H took to Twitter/X to share his love for the couple and promote the show.

“Excited for you to see the @BiancaBelairWWE & @MontezFordWWE that all of us at @WWE are so fortunate to know behind the scenes.

Not only are they incredible athletes and bonafide Superstars, but they’re also two of the best human beings you’ll meet.

See for yourself… #LoveAndWWE: Bianca & Montez is now streaming on @hulu.“

In his tweet, Triple H complimented the duo for not only being incredible athletes but also “two of the best human beings.” Fans are surely excited to take a peak at the power couple’s life beyond wrestling.

Meanwhile, Bianca had an awesome performance last week at WWE Women’s Royal Rumble. She lasted an impressive 47-minutes in the ring before being eliminated by Bayley, who went on to win the title. This Rumble featured some notable entrants, including a returning Naomi and a debuting Jade Cargill. Stay tuned to RingsideNews for more!
