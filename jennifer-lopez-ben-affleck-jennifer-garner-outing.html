Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck Enjoy a Night Out With Jennifer Garner

Jennifer Lopez, Ben Affleck, and Jennifer Garner are all one big happy family.

On Sunday evening, the trio enjoyed a family outing in Los Angeles. Accompanying them were Affleck and Garner’s kids, 15-year-old daughter Seraphina and 11-year-old son Samuel, and Lopez’s 15-year-old child Emme, whom she shares with ex-husband Marc Anthony.

For her outfit of the night, Lopez looked cool in an all-white monochromatic look, which consisted of a simple long-sleeved top and a baggy wide-leg pants. She accessorized with her signature hoop earrings and, for a pop of color, a maroon leather shoulder bag. Meanwhile, Affleck looked casual in a red and gray flannel button-down, a gray T-shirt, khaki pants, and red and white Nike sneakers.

In other photos, which you can see here, Garner displayed her off-duty style in a beige knit sweater, straight-leg jeans, and black sneakers.

BACKGRID

Affleck and Garner, who divorced in 2018 after 13 years of marriage, are known to have a friendly co-parenting relationship, and have often been spotted out together since their split. (Garner is dating businessman John Miller, whom she has been romantically linked to since her marriage to Affleck ended.)

The exes blended their family with Lopez’s when Affleck and Lopez officially married in 2022.

“That’s almost five teenagers,” Lopez reflected during a guest appearance on Live With Kelly and Mark last year. “The youngest is 11, so he’s not quite [a teen], but preteen. He’s an angel.”

She added, “The teenage years are tough. They start challenging you and everything. You have this baby for a while and then it’s like your best little friend who loves being with you all the time, and all of a sudden they’re like, ‘Get out of my room.’”
