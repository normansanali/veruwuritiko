Logan Paul Beats Dillon Danis, Calls Out Rey Mysterio

Logan Paul has his sights set on Rey Mysterio.

Logan Paul returned to boxing at The Prime Card on October 14, and he battled Dillon Danis. Paul controlled the action and ultimately scored the win. Danis got himself disqualified with just a few seconds left in the bout, and Paul clinched the victory via decision.

After the fight, Paul made it clear that he intends to return to WWE so he can challenge Rey Mysterio for the WWE United States Championship. Paul then challenged Conor McGregor to face him in a boxing match.

Rey Mysterio then responded on social media, as he told Paul that he’s easy to find every week on WWE SmackDown.

Logan Paul previously made his WWE in-ring debut at WrestleMania 38; he and The Miz defeated Rey and Dominik Mysterio in a tag team match.

Paul last wrestled at WWE SummerSlam on August 5, and there is no word on when he will return to WWE.

WrestleZone will provide more information as it becomes available.
