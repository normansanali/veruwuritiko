Dennis McKinley Biography | Dennis Mckinley Businessperson

Dennis McKinley is an American serial entrepreneur and fiance to television personality, and model Porsha Williams. McKinley is a Detroit native.

McKinley is the CEO of a business conglomerate Detroit Equities site. The company runs the Atlanta-based businesses Cru Hookah Lounge and the Original Hot Dog Factory. His businesses also have locations in his hometown.

Dennis studied economics at the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor, Michigan. According to his website, McKinley has a background in real estate, e-commerce, retail, business and product development.”

Dennis Mckinley Hot Dog | Dennis Mckinley Hot Dog Factory

McKinley is the brains behind The Original Hot Dog Factory. Dennis operates a location in the ATL and one in nearby Smyrna, Georgia, as well as a food truck and catering operation. The Original Hot Dog Company serves a wide array of proteins (including vegetarian options), but the bestseller is the Detroit Coney Dog, a beef hot dog topped with chili, shredded cheese and chopped onions that’s popular in Dennis’ hometown. The menu now also features an array of healthy salads to encourage McKinley’s baby vegan efforts — and to balance her sweet tooth when she wants to go for the deep fried Oreos and Twinkies or funnel cake sticks for dessert.
